## 中文解釋 (Chinese Explanation)

市場經濟是指主要通過市場機制調節經濟活動的經濟體制。供給與需求的平衡決定了價格，企業和個人為了獲取利潤而進行經濟活動。

### 主要特徵：
- 建立在私有財產制基礎上
- 競爭是經濟的核心動力
- 保障個人和企業的經濟自由
- 價格機制決定資源的有效配置

## English Explanation

Market economy is an economic system in which economic activities are primarily coordinated through market mechanisms. Price is determined by the balance of supply and demand, and businesses and individuals engage in economic activities to pursue profit.

### Key Characteristics:
- Based on private property rights
- Competition serves as the central driving force
- Guarantees economic freedom for individuals and enterprises
- Price mechanism determines the efficient allocation of resources
